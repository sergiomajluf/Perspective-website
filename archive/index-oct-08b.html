Perspective

How are we online?

What is this

Perspective is a research project on how we use the Internet, and about our awareness of that usage and participation. Technology has the proven power to influence our behaviors, so we set to the task of investigating to what extent that influence can be measured and experienced.

Perspective is under development within the context of Designing for Persuasion, a graduate class at the Master in Interactive Telecommunications Program, New York University.
